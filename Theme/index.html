The design thinking process
 
 
The design thinking process is best thought of as the overlapping intersection of three spaces: the human, the viable, and the technical.

As you might expect, the human aspect is the creative aspect of the process (How do people respond differently to different ideas?) The viable relates to desirability of an idea within a business environment. The technical is the actual implementation of the human and business aspects.
